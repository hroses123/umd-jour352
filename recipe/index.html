4-Ingredient Crispy Tofu

With just 4 ingredients and 5 minutes of active prep, this easy and crunchy tofu is an absolute protein lifesaver on busy nights! Enjoy it simply, just thrown into a salad … or create a power bowl featuring your favorite toppings and rice!

Why you’ll love this recipe:

  • VEGAN: This recipe is completely animal-product free.
  • GLUTEN-FREE: This recipe contains absolutely zero gluten products.
  • FAST AND EASY: Besides for the time that the tofu needs to be pressed, this recipe will take all of 5 minutes to prepare.
  • PERSONAL: This recipe can be tailored to any dietary preferencees. Mix this tofu with any sauce, dressing, or base for a delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of super firm tofu
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Garlic power to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees farnheit
  2. Press the tofu with something heavy on top of it for an hour to remove the excess water.
  3. Slice the tofu in half the long way.
  4. Cut the tofu into 1/2 inch cubes
  5. Toss the tofu with the cornstarch
  6. Lay the tofu on a baking sheet
  7. Drizzle the tofu with olive oil
  8. Sprinkle the tofu with garlic salt or any other desired spieces.
  9. Bake the tofu for 30 minutes.
  10. Turn the oven off and leave the tofu in the oven for an extra ten minutes.

Tips

  • SHOP AHEAD: Freeze the tofu in advance for a more chickeny and chewy texture.
  • LEFTOVERS: Store the extra tofu in an airtight container in the fridge and enjoy for up to a week.
